About Us

JOVIKA FOODS is a leading food manufacturing company based in Makurdi, Benue State - Nigeria's food basket. We specialize in producing high-quality food products using the finest locally-sourced ingredients and modern manufacturing processes. Our commitment to excellence, food safety, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in Nigerian households. Every product that leaves our facility at C34/35 Hudco Quarters North Bank Makurdi meets rigorous quality standards, ensuring that families across Nigeria enjoy wholesome, nutritious, and delicious food products they can trust.

Our Products & Services

At JOVIKA FOODS, we manufacture a diverse range of quality food products designed to meet the needs of modern Nigerian families. Our product line includes carefully processed grains, flours, seasonings, and packaged food items - all produced under strict hygiene conditions. Quality Assurance

When you choose JOVIKA FOODS, you're choosing products manufactured with integrity, care, and unwavering commitment to excellence.
