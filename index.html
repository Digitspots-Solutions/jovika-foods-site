About Us

WM Logic Family Office Limited, also operating as WM Logic Heritage Wealth Co, is a premier financial services institution based in Makurdi, Benue State. As a licensed bureau de change operator, we specialize in foreign exchange transactions while providing comprehensive family office services to high-net-worth individuals and families across Nigeria.

Our commitment is to preserve and grow generational wealth through strategic financial planning, transparent currency exchange services, and personalized wealth management solutions. With our deep understanding of the Nigerian financial landscape and global currency markets, we serve as your dedicated partner in achieving financial security and prosperity.

Located at C34/35 Hudco Quarters North Bank, Makurdi, we combine international financial standards with local market expertise to deliver exceptional service to our valued clients.
